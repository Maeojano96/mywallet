Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 12, 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you use MyWallet:

  • Account Info: Email address, name, and profile photo (if using Google Sign-In)
  • Financial Data: Transactions, budgets, debts, and salary information you enter
  • Device Info: Basic device information for app functionality

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain the MyWallet service
  • Sync your data across devices (when signed in)
  • Improve app features and user experience
  • Send important notifications about your account

3. Data Storage & Security

Local Storage: When not signed in, all data is stored locally on your device only.

Cloud Storage: When signed in with Google or Email, data is encrypted and stored in Firebase Cloud.

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data. However, no method is 100% secure.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share data only:

  • With your consent
  • To comply with legal requirements
  • To protect our rights and safety

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and update your personal information
  • Export your data at any time
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Opt out of non-essential communications

6. Cookies & Tracking

MyWallet does not use tracking cookies. We only use local storage to save your preferences and data on your device.

7. Children's Privacy

MyWallet is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or email.

9. Contact Us
