Support & Help

How do I add a transaction?
Tap the + button (floating button) on the home screen, select "Transaction", enter the amount, choose type (income/expense), select a category, and tap "Add Transaction". You can also go to the Transactions page and tap the + icon.
How do I set a budget?
On the home screen, find the "Weekly Budgets" section. Tap any budget category to edit its weekly limit. Enter your desired amount and tap "Set Budget". The progress bar will show how much you've spent.
How do I add my salary?
On the home screen, scroll to the "Salary Income" card and tap "Add Monthly Salary". Enter your monthly salary amount and tap "Save Salary". This amount will be included in your total balance calculation.
Can I use the app without signing in?
Yes! You can use MyWallet in Demo Mode without creating an account. Your data will be saved locally on your device. However, signing in allows you to sync data across devices and access cloud backup.
How do I export my data?
Go to Settings and tap "Export Data". Your data will be downloaded as a JSON file. You can later import this file to restore your data on any device.
How do I change the currency?
Go to Settings, tap "Currency", select your preferred currency (IQD, USD, EUR, GBP, or TRY), and tap "Save Currency". All amounts will update automatically.
Is my financial data secure?
Yes! When signed in, your data is encrypted and stored in Firebase Cloud. When using locally, data stays on your device only. We never sell or share your financial information with third parties.
How do I delete my account?
Go to Settings and tap "Reset All Data" to clear your financial data. To delete your account completely, please contact us at maroduhoki6@gmail.com.
