Support & Help Center

Find answers to common questions
How do I add a transaction?
Tap the + button (floating button) on the home screen, select "Transaction", enter the amount, choose type (income/expense), select a category, and tap "Add Transaction".
How do I set a budget?
On the home screen, find the "Weekly Budgets" section. Tap any budget category to edit its weekly limit. Enter your desired amount and tap "Set Budget".
How do I add my salary?
On the home screen, scroll to the "Salary Income" card and tap "Add Monthly Salary". Enter your monthly salary amount and tap "Save Salary".
Can I use the app without signing in?
Yes! You can use MyWallet in Demo Mode without creating an account. Your data will be saved locally on your device. Signing in allows you to sync data across devices.
How do I export my data?
Go to Settings and tap "Export Data". Your data will be downloaded as a JSON file. You can later import this file to restore your data.
How do I change the currency?
Go to Settings, tap "Currency", select your preferred currency (IQD, USD, EUR, GBP, or TRY), and tap "Save Currency".
Is my financial data secure?
Yes! When signed in, your data is encrypted and stored in Firebase Cloud. When using locally, data stays on your device only. We never sell or share your financial information.
How do I delete my account?
Go to Settings and tap "Reset All Data" to clear your financial data. To delete your account completely, please contact us at maroduhoki6@gmail.com.
